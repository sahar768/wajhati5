Seef Corniche

The Corniche Al-Saif is one of the top tourist destinations in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia. The Corniche is a renowned spot for leisurely strolls and enjoying the breathtaking sea views and recreational activities. Stretching along the western coast of Jeddah, the Corniche Al-Saif features beautiful parks, green spaces, and pedestrian pathways offering opportunities for walking or jogging by the sea.

Waterfall Park

The Waterfall Park in Jeddah is one of the prominent tourist destinations in Saudi Arabia. The park is an ideal place for families and friends to spend a fun and relaxing day. The Waterfall Park is characterized by a beautiful artificial waterfall that adds a sense of beauty and tranquility to the place. Additionally, the park features vast green spaces and gardens filled with plants and trees that provide an ideal environment for picnics and relaxation.

Fakih Aquarium

Fakieh Aquarium is a marine museum and wildlife park located in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia. It is one of the largest and most modern tourist attractions in the region, attracting visitors from within and outside the kingdom. The museum features a diverse range of tanks housing various marine creatures, including colorful fish, coral reefs, large fish such as sharks and sea turtles, and many other marine organisms.
